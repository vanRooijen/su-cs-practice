## Broadband Networks and Applications

The research page includes the Stellenbosch unit of the Telkom-Siemens Centre of Excellence in ATM and Broadband Networks and their Applications.

Its stated aims include:

- promoting R&D in broadband technologies and applications
- training postgraduate students and professionals in telecommunications expertise
