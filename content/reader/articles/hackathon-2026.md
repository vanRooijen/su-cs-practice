## 2026 SUDS Div and Conquer Web Development Hackathon

Hey developer, we are announcing a hackathon.

More information at <https://hackathon.devsoc.co.za>.

### Hackathon Opening Event

Friday, 27 February 2026, 5:30pm at Math/Industrial 1005.

Join us for an evening where we open DevSoc's first hackathon of 2026. Ask questions or find a team. If you cannot make it, you can still submit. We have over R8000 in cash prizes.

Sign up for official communications via the MS form on the website or by scanning your student card at the opening event.

### Submissions

Teams must email the official submission form to `devsoc@sun.ac.za` by 3 March at 11:59pm.

Full guide: <https://hackathon.devsoc.co.za/submissions>

### Rulebook

<https://hackathon.devsoc.co.za/rulebook>

Please note submissions are only allowed on Cloudflare Pages, GitHub Pages, Vercel, WordPress, or Drupal.
