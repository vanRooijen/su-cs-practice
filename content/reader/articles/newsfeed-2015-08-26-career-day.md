<div class="newsitem"><h1>Computer Science Career Day 2015</h1><div class="newsdate"><span class="day"> Wednesday </span><span class="date"> 26 </span><span class="month">August</span><span class="year">2015</span></div><p>The Computer Science Department at Stellenbosch University receives many enquiries from industry regarding job opportunities for Computer Science students who are to graduate. We have an event annually where companies are in direct contact with these students to discuss job opportunities.</p><p>The event takes the format of a Careers Day where each company sets up branded stations and where their representatives will be available to chat to the students. Computer Science students are the only students who attend this event. This year the event took place on Friday, 21 August 2015 in the Vrouevereniging-Hall in the Neelsie, Stellenbosch.</p></div>
