<div class="newsitem"><h1>Collaboration with TU Braunschweig, Germany</h1><div class="newsdate"><span class="day"> Wednesday </span><span class="date"> 25 </span><span class="month">April</span><span class="year">2018</span></div><p>The Faculty of Science has recently signed a memorandum of understanding with the <a href="https://www.tu-braunschweig.de/fk1">Carl-Friedrich-Gauß-Fakultät</a> of the Technical University of Braunschweig, Germany, to establish a close international collaboration in computer science.</p><p>Prof Wolf-Tilo Bahlke, the dean of the Carl-Friedrich-Gauß-Fakultät of the Technical University Braunschweig in Germany, and Prof Ina Schaefer (Dean of Studies) recently visited the Faculty of Science to sign a memorandum of understanding between both faculties. The goal of both faculties is to establish a close international collaboration in computer science that includes joint projects and staff and student exchanges.</p><p class="noimage colimage"><img src="/cs-assets/newsfeed/IMG_6514.JPG" alt=" TUBS "></p><p>Back from left to right: Prof Ina Schaefer (Dean of Studies, Carl-Friedrich-Gauß-Fakultät, TUBS ), Prof Bernd Fischer (Head of Computer Science, SU), Prof Bruce Watson (Head of Information Sciences, SU) Front from left to right: Prof Wolf-Tilo Bahlke (Dean, Carl-Friedrich-Gauß-Fakultät, TUBS), Prof Louise Warnich (Dean, Faculty of Science, SU)</p></div>
