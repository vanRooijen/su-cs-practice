<div class="newsitem"><h1>Jane Street Depth First Learning fellows</h1><div class="newsdate"><span class="day"> Monday </span><span class="date"> 15 </span><span class="month">April</span><span class="year">2019</span></div><p>Dr. Steve Kroon has been selected as one of the inaugaural Jane Street Depth First Learning fellows. Depth First Learning is an initiative to develop lesson plans building up to mastery of significant research papers while running study groups on the required material.
