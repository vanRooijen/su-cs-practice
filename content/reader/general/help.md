## Help

This website works like a browser-based desktop workspace.

### Quick Start

- Open **Home** for the landing page.
- Open **Articles** for news and updates.
- Open **People / Programs / Research / About** from the top bar.

### Window Basics

- The URL decides which app and content route is active.
- Apps stay open until you close their window.
- Sidebar entries represent open windows across tabs.
- Click a sidebar entry to focus it.
- Click the focused sidebar entry to minimize it.

### Desktop vs Phone Controls

- Desktop: right-click a link for **Open in New App Window** or **Open in New Browser Tab**.
- Phone: tap and hold a link for the same options.
- Phone: use the bottom bar **Tabs** button to switch windows.
