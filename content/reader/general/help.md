## Help

This website works like a desktop workspace.

- The URL decides which app and content route is active.
- Apps stay open until you close their window.
- Sidebar entries represent open windows.
- Right-click anywhere and choose **Help** to return to this page.

If you are new here, start on Home and open articles from the landing page cards.
