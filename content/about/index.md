## About the Computer Science Division

The Computer Science Division is part of the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Stellenbosch University.

The current website describes the division as combining foundational computer science with practical software, systems, and data-focused training and research.

Use the sections in this app for:

- department overview
- contact information
- visit and location details
