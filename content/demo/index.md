## Demo Mission

Do the steps in order. Keep this page open in one tab while you run the drill.

<details open>
  <summary><strong>Step 1: Use Browser Tabs On Purpose</strong></summary>

Open the next step in a **new browser tab**:

1. Right-click this link: [Step 2 Page](/relay/pull-over)
2. Choose **Open in New Browser Tab**

This proves we are adding workflow, not replacing browser features.
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>Step 3: Forward Navigation Preview</strong></summary>

After Step 2, you will use browser history to move around:

- Step 4 will send you back here with **Back**
- Step 5 will send you forward again with **Forward**

You are testing normal browser history, not a fake in-app version.
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>Step 5: Use Browser Forward</strong></summary>

After Step 4 sends you back here:

1. Press your browser **Forward** button once
2. You should return to the Step 2 page
3. Come back here again (Back is fine)
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>Step 6: Open The Final Step In A New App Window</strong></summary>

Now test in-tab windowing:

1. Right-click this link: [Step 7 Final](/demo/final)
2. Choose **Open in New App Window**
</details>
