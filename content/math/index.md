## Mathematics Playground

This app demonstrates LaTeX rendering directly inside markdown content.

- Open **Linear Algebra** for matrices, eigenvalues, and vector spaces.
- Open **Calculus** for derivatives, integrals, and series.
- Open **Probability** for distributions and expectations.

Inline math works: $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$.

Block math works:

$$
\int_0^1 x^2\,dx = \frac{1}{3}
$$

And larger equations:

$$
\nabla \cdot \vec{E} = \frac{\rho}{\varepsilon_0}, \qquad
\nabla \times \vec{E} = -\frac{\partial \vec{B}}{\partial t}
$$
