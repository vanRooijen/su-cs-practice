<div id="content" class="pretext-content"><section class="section" id="section-crypto-public"><h2 class="heading hide-type">
<span class="type">Section</span> <span class="codenumber">4.3</span> <span class="title">Public-key primitives</span>
</h2>
<p id="p-284">Consider a cryptographic scheme consisting of the sets of encryption and decryption transformations \(E_e\) and \(D_d\) with \(e,d\in \mathcal{K}\text{,}\) respectively.</p>
<p id="p-285">The encryption scheme is public-key if for each associated encryption/decryption key pair \((e, d)\text{,}\) if given \(e\) it is infeasible to determine the corresponding decryption key \(d\text{.}\)</p>
