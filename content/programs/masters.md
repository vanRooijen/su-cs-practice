## Masters in Computer Science

The Masters page presents the degree as a two-year thesis-based programme intended to train students to evaluate and conduct world-class research.

### Entry guidance from source

- Honours-level background in Computer Science (or approved equivalent)
- Supervisor agreement is essential before applying
- Applicants are encouraged to review research areas and publications before approaching supervisors

Source: https://cs.sun.ac.za/teaching/masters/
