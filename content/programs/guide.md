## Programme Guide Highlights

The programme guide notes that Computer Science at Stellenbosch focuses on software development and that students should always verify final module combinations in the official university calendar.

### Full Computer Science curriculum routes

Programmes/focal areas listed with a full CS curriculum include:

- BSc Computer Science (General CS, Computer Systems, Data Science)
- BDatSci (Computer Science focal area)
- Select Mathematical Sciences and related focal areas listed on the guide page

### Curriculum structure (core)

The guide highlights this core progression:

- First year: CS114, CS144
- Second year: CS214, CS244
- Third year: CS343, CS344, plus options such as CS313/CS314/CS315 and CS345

### Important note

The page explicitly warns that programme/module combinations may change and must be confirmed in the calendar/yearbook.
