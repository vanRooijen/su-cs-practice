## Prospective Postgraduate Students

The postgraduate page states that admission is competitive and selection-based, and that minimum requirements do not guarantee admission.

### Key points

- Honours selection typically expects a strong CS and mathematics background
- Masters/PhD applicants should first secure supervisor agreement
- Applications are submitted through official Stellenbosch postgraduate channels
- Part-time and international pathways are supported with specific constraints

### Contact

The page directs specific postgraduate queries to `postgrad@cs.sun.ac.za`.
