## Honours in Computer Science

The Honours page describes the degree as a one-year full-time postgraduate programme that combines core CS theory with modern application-focused modules.

### Structure

- 32-credit honours project
- Six elective modules of 16 credits each
- Strong expectation of independent work and time management

The page positions Honours as preparation for MSc studies and advanced industry roles.

Source: https://cs.sun.ac.za/teaching/honours/
