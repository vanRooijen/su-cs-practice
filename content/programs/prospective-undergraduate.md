## Prospective Undergraduate Students

The prospective undergraduate page describes Computer Science as the theory and practice of algorithms, combining design, analysis, and engineering.

### Why study Computer Science?

The source highlights broad demand for computing skills and frames CS as both practically valuable and creatively rewarding.

### Why Stellenbosch?

The page emphasizes:

- Academic excellence and research strength
- A strong student community and campus-town integration

### Course shape

- Year 1: programming foundations
- Year 2: data structures/algorithms and architecture
- Year 3: concurrency, databases/web systems, networks, software engineering, and advanced electives
