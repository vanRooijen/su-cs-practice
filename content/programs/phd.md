## PhD in Computer Science

The PhD page describes a three-year dissertation-focused degree requiring original, specialised research.

### Entry guidance from source

- Masters-level qualification in Computer Science (or approved equivalent)
- Supervisor agreement required before applying
- Strong emphasis on proposing, planning, executing, and publishing research

Source: https://cs.sun.ac.za/teaching/phd/
